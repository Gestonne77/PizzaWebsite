PIZZA TYPES

The star of the show, pepperoni, is a type of cured and seasoned dry sausage made from beef or a combination of beef and pork. It is thinly sliced and placed evenly across the pizza. The pepperoni slices are known for their slightly spicy and smoky flavor, and they become crispy and slightly curled at the edges when baked.
